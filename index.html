About Our Club

Chartered on August 14, 2024, under Rotaract International District 3191, we are more than a service organization—we are a community of passionate changemakers driven by purpose and united by action.

Through impactful service initiatives, leadership development, professional networking and meaningful fellowship, we empower young adults to create lasting change while growing into leaders who inspire their communities.

A Message from the President

🪷

"Some journeys are measured by milestones. Others are remembered by the lives they touch."

Welcome to the Rotaract Club of Bangalore High Grounds — A community where service finds its purpose, leadership discovers its meaning, and every individual has the opportunity to become part of something greater than themselves.

This Rotary Year, inspired by Create Lasting Impact and guided by our vision, ETCH THE ETERNAL – Create What Time Cannot Erase, we believe that our greatest achievement will never be the number of projects we complete, but the legacy we leave in the hearts of people and the communities we serve.

As you explore our journey, I hope you see not just the work we do, but the values we live by. Together, let us inspire, serve, and create a future that will be remembered long after today.

Because some stories fade with time...
The best ones are etched forever.

Rtr. Nandan Kumar V S

President • Rotaract Club of Bangalore High Grounds

Areas of Focus

Rotary's Seven Areas of Focus guide our projects and initiatives.

🪷
🏥

Disease Prevention

Health camps, awareness drives, and preventive care initiatives for underserved communities.

💧

Water & Sanitation

Clean water access, sanitation projects, and hygiene education in rural and urban areas.

👩‍🏫

Basic Education

Literacy programs, school supply drives, and mentorship for underprivileged students.

🤱

Maternal & Child Health

Supporting maternal care, nutrition programs, and child welfare initiatives.

☮️

Peace & Conflict

Building understanding, interfaith dialogues, and conflict resolution workshops.

💼

Economic Development

Skill development, entrepreneurship workshops, and livelihood support programs.

🌿

Environment

Tree planting drives, sustainability campaigns, and environmental awareness activities.

Creating Meaningful Impact

Every project we undertake is driven by compassion, collaboration, and purpose.

🪷
📚 Education Drive

Vidya Daan — School Supply Distribution

School supply distribution and mentorship program for underprivileged students across government schools in Bangalore.

🏥 Health Camp

Swasthya — Community Health Camp

Free health checkup camp providing medical consultations, eye testing, and dental care to 200+ beneficiaries.

🌿 Green Drive

Harithalaya — Tree Plantation Drive

Tree planting initiative across Cubbon Park and surrounding areas, with 500+ saplings planted by our volunteers.
