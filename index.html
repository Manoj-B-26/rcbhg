About Our Club

Established in 2024, the Rotaract Club of Bangalore High Grounds is a community-based club sponsored by the Rotary Club of Bangalore High Grounds under Rotary International District 3191.

Since our charter, we have been committed to creating opportunities for young adults to develop leadership skills, serve communities, and build meaningful friendships through impactful service initiatives and professional development.

A Message from the President

🪷

"Welcome to the Rotaract Club of Bangalore High Grounds. Our club is more than an organization—it is a family of passionate individuals united by a shared purpose to serve, lead, and inspire."

Every initiative we undertake reflects our commitment to creating meaningful impact while fostering leadership, innovation, and lifelong friendships.

Areas of Focus

Rotary's Seven Areas of Focus guide our projects and initiatives.

🪷
🏥

Disease Prevention

Health camps, awareness drives, and preventive care initiatives for underserved communities.

💧

Water & Sanitation

Clean water access, sanitation projects, and hygiene education in rural and urban areas.

👩‍🏫

Basic Education

Literacy programs, school supply drives, and mentorship for underprivileged students.

🤱

Maternal & Child Health

Supporting maternal care, nutrition programs, and child welfare initiatives.

☮️

Peace & Conflict

Building understanding, interfaith dialogues, and conflict resolution workshops.

💼

Economic Development

Skill development, entrepreneurship workshops, and livelihood support programs.

🌿

Environment

Tree planting drives, sustainability campaigns, and environmental awareness activities.

Creating Meaningful Impact

Every project we undertake is driven by compassion, collaboration, and purpose.

🪷
📚 Education Drive

Vidya Daan — School Supply Distribution

School supply distribution and mentorship program for underprivileged students across government schools in Bangalore.

🏥 Health Camp

Swasthya — Community Health Camp

Free health checkup camp providing medical consultations, eye testing, and dental care to 200+ beneficiaries.

🌿 Green Drive

Harithalaya — Tree Plantation Drive

Tree planting initiative across Cubbon Park and surrounding areas, with 500+ saplings planted by our volunteers.
